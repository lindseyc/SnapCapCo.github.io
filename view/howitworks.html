How It Works

SnapCap uses deep learning to analyze your mood in the photo. We’ve trained a resnet-30 neural net on thousands of images of different moods, all classified by the emotions exhibited by the people in those photos. So, we run your image through our neural net, and get an emotional analysis of your face as it appears in the photo!

Next, we calculate a song that best fits your mood, using factors of songs such as their tempo, danceability, and genre. From that song, we pull a line from that song’s lyrics to caption your photo!

What's a neural net?

Neural Nets are trained by detecting features in an input, for example a photo. Given images that are labeled with a mood, the model learns to generalize its predictions based on this training data. Our neural net has an error rate of ____. It makes a prediction given its learning of features from the __ moods it was trained on. These are "happy," "sad," "surprise," "anger," and "disgust."
